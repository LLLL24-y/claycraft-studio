Working with clay is one of the oldest, most rewarding forms of artistic expression, connecting the creator directly to the earth to mold, shape, and transform a simple lump into functional art or sculpture. Whether you are a beginner interested in simple, air-dry projects or an experienced artist diving into pottery, understanding the properties of clay and the techniques to work with it is essential. Here is a guide to working with clay, suitable for a, ceramics, or crafting website.
